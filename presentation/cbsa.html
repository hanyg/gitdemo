Executive Summary

The ESR, is a strategic blueprint:

  • a guide for implementing a digital transformation

    • improve the Customer Experience

    • align the LOB

    • enhance reporting

  • Agency change management plan;

Purpose

  • ESR is:

    • planning tool

    • visual roadmap

  • Stakeholders Buy-In

    • Big Picture.

    • Conveys the transformation

    • improves the Customer Experience.

    • Depicts how productivity will improve.

  • Sharing Details

    • Multiple views

      • Strategic thinkers.

      • Tactical implementers.

    • Timelines of the transformation.
